# The Whispering Moth

A moth the size of a dinner plate with wings that look like blinking eyes.

## Harmony Classification

**"Psionic Lepidoptera"**

A hallucinogenic insect. Its dust causes temporary psychosis. Protocol: Wear filtration masks.

## What Players Know

The Academy warns students to wear masks in moth-heavy areas.

### Clan Lore (Universal): "The Liar-Bug"

They mimic the voices of loved ones to lure travelers off cliffs.

**If you hear your name called in the fog, cover your ears.** If you answer, they steal your voice.

### Recent Incident

Lucky (Squad 09) claims he heard his little sister calling from a ventilation shaft in the Deep-Hull. He was found clawing at a sealed grate, hallucinating that she was trapped inside.
