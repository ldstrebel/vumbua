# Rill

**Role:** Mizizi Researcher / "Traitor" / Futurist
**Affiliation:** Harmony, formerly Mizizi (Left 2 years ago)
**Status:** Active
**Archetype:** The Futurist

## Overview
Rill is a Mizizi who left the Forest two years ago to join Harmony. She is viewed as a traitor or "Lost One" by traditional Mizizi, but her motivations are altruistic.

## Personality
- **Intelligent, Determined:** She loves her forest, but knows the Elders' "Stagnation" doctrine is suicide.
- **Goal:** Joined Harmony to find a cure for the "Eternal Growth" cancer/data corruption affecting the Mizizi, even if it means defying her family.
- **Connection:** Knows Britt and Aggie (possibly).
