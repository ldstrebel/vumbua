# Celia Vance

**Role:** Operations Director
**Affiliation:** Vumbua Academy
**Status:** Active

## Overview
Celia Vance manages the day-to-day logistics of Vumbua Academy. She creates the schedules, manages housing, and ensures the "Engine Room" of the school keeps running.

## Personality
- Efficient, stressed, and constantly juggling a dozen datapads.
- Less ideological than Dean Vane or Hesperus; she just wants things to *work*.

## Mechanics
- Often the bearer of bad news regarding resources or scheduling conflicts.
