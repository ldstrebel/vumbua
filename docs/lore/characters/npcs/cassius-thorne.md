# Cassius Thorne

**Role:** Squad 01 (The Echelon) - Member (Gold Rank)
**Affiliation:** Harmony (Thorne Family), Vumbua Academy
**Status:** Active

## Overview
Cassius Thorne is the "muscle" of Squad 01, from the Thorne family (known for exploration and scouting). He is quiet, dangerously competent, and follows Valerius's lead.

## Personality
- Loyal, stoic, and observant.
- Less vocal than Valerius, but equally committed to Harmony's ideals.
- **Relation to Silas Thorne?** Possible distant relation to "Old Man Silas" (Squad 05), likely representing the branch of the family that stayed "respectable."
