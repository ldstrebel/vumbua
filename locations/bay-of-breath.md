# Bay of Breath

> The empire's lungs — an algae colony that produces and purifies breathable air.

| | |

## Overview

The Bay of Breath is home to an algae colony that produces and purifies breathable air. Essential for deep-hull operations, submarine expeditions, and enclosed environments. It is a critical life-support resource for Harmony's fleet and frontier operations.
