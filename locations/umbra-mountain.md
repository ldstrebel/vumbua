# Umbra Mountain

> The home mountain of House Vox and source of every crystal battery in Harmony.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Major Node City |
| **Node Resource** | Umber Crystals |
| **Faction** | House Vox (The Spark) |
| **Node Rating** | Major (+100 to +800 amps) |
| **Status** | Core Harmony territory |

## Overview

Umbra Mountain is the source of the umber crystals used in every generation of Vox crystal batteries, from the first post-Night-of-Sparks surge protectors to the current Panda line. The mountain is heavily mined and industrialised, with House Vox controlling all extraction and refinement operations.
