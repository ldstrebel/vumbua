# Elysium

> Desert sand that shows you what you most desire — beautiful, dangerous, and strictly controlled.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Node City |
| **Node Resource** | Snow Sand |
| **Node Rating** | Moderate (+30 to +100 amps) |
| **Status** | Integrated (strictly controlled) |

## Overview

Elysium produces Snow Sand — desert sand that causes vivid hallucinations and illusions of whatever the affected person most desires. Highly dangerous and highly regulated. The resource is used in medicine and interrogation, but its potential for abuse makes it one of Harmony's most controlled substances.
