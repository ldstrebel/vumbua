# Block 12

Block 12 is the classic Academy dormitory housing within [[vumbua-academy|Vumbua Academy]]. It was assigned to [[iggy|Iggy]] and [[ignatius|Ignatius]] during intake.

## What Players Know

Standard dorm block — the "classic" Academy housing style. When [[ignatius|Ignatius]] learned that [[iggy|Iggy]] was his roommate, he tried to lead them both to the room, but [[iggy|Iggy]] immediately wandered off to explore instead.
