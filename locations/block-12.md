# Block 12

Block 12 is the classic Academy dormitory housing within [[Vumbua Academy]]. It was assigned to [[Iggy]] and [[Ignatius]] during intake.

## What Players Know

Standard dorm block — the "classic" Academy housing style. When [[Ignatius]] learned that [[Iggy]] was his roommate, he tried to lead them both to the room, but [[Iggy]] immediately wandered off to explore instead.
