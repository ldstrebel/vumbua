# Prima

> Clean water from thin air — survival in a crystal.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Node City |
| **Node Resource** | Dew Crystals |
| **Node Rating** | Moderate (+30 to +100 amps) |

## Overview

Prima produces Dew Crystals — crystals that condense water from ambient air, producing roughly a cup of clean water each. Invaluable in desert regions and aboard ships where fresh water is scarce. Prima is an essential survival resource for frontier operations.
