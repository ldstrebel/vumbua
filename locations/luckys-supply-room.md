## Overview
A cluttered, dimly lit bunker tucked away in the maintenance and boiler sections of the Academy. This is [[Lucky]]'s private storeroom and "interrogation chamber." It is filled with wooden crates of "acquired" goods, strange aetheric tools, smuggled magical components, and contraband. Warm brass accents catch the low light, giving it the vibe of a mysterious inventor's bunker.

## Session Appearances

### Session 4
- The party (Iggy, Lomi, Ignatius) is pulled in here by [[Lucky]] after he sees Iggy's golden entrance exam receipt.
- [[Sarge]] is present, acting as muscle (though mostly just keeping an eye on Lucky).
- The interrogation is interrupted by **Dash**, an eccentric student with an "in" for Resonance Race merchandise.

## Lore & Secrets
- **Contraband Hub:** This room contains items that technically shouldn't exist within the strict Academy supply chain.
- **The "RR" Connection:** The room also serves as a staging ground for dealing in illicit "rr" (Resonance Race) merchandise.
