# Feltland

> Harmony's agricultural backbone — a vast plain of supercharged soil.

| | |
|---|---|

## Overview

Feltland is a vast plain near Harmony Prime. The soil is extraordinarily fertile — essentially supercharged fertiliser that accelerates growth far beyond natural rates. It serves as the agricultural backbone of the empire, feeding Harmony's population.
