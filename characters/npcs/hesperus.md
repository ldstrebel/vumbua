# Senior Exploranaut Hesperus

**Role:** Senior Exploranaut / Instructor
**Affiliation:** [[vumbua-academy|Vumbua Academy]], The Guilds
**Status:** Active

## Overview
A veteran explorer and instructor who leads the practical field training for students. He is tough, scarred, and has survived the "Wilds" outside the Academy's safe zones.

## Personality
- Gruff, mentoring, and practical.
- Values survival and adaptability over theoretical knowledge.
- Has a grudging respect for the resourcefulness of Rust-tier students.
