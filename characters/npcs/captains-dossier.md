## ***üïµÔ∏è Key Captain Dossiers & Secret Agendas***

### ***1\. Valentine Sterling (The Glory Hound)***

* ***The Pitch:** "Join the elite. We have the best food, the best press, and the least chance of death."*  
* ***The Secret:** He isn't actually exploring. He's re-visiting a "Safe Node" discovered 20 years ago and pretending it's new for the cameras.*  
* ***The Risk:** Zero danger, but zero respect from real explorers.*

### ***2\. Kaelo "Ghost" Vane (The Spy)***

* ***The Pitch:** "The Academy needs eyes, not heroes. Help me see what the River Clan is hiding."*  
* ***The Secret:** Kaelo suspects the River Clan has a weapon that can ground the Sky-Spire.*  
* ***The Risk:** If caught, the Academy will disavow you. You will be left behind.*

### ***3\. Ignis "Scorch" (The Daredevil)***

* ***The Pitch:** "We are going to run the engine at 120% until it melts or we win."*  
* ***The Secret:** His engine is melting. He needs an Elemental (Ignatius) or a Mechanic (Loami) to physically hold it together during the climb.*  
* ***The Risk:** High probability of explosion. High probability of glory.*

### ***4\. Elara Thorne (The Saint)***

* ***The Pitch:** "We are bringing food and medicine to the 'Rust' populations stranded outside the gates."*  
* ***The Secret:** She is smuggling unregistered "Rust" candidates back into the city disguised as patients.*  
* ***The Risk:** You become an accomplice to treason against the Loom's sorting protocols.*

### ***5\. Professor Althea Ink (The Xenobotanist)***

* ***The Pitch:** "The Mizizi fear the 'Old Growth' because they say it whispers. I say it reads. Help me prove the forest is a library before the Verdant Trust mulches it."*  
* ***The Secret:** She is secretly working with **Rill** (the River-Born adoptee). Rill provides the coordinates; Ink provides the ship. They are trying to extract a "Core Sample" from an Ancient Tree to prove it contains data, not just wood.*  
* ***The Risk:** The "Whispers" in the Old Growth are psychic data-dumps. Low-Willpower characters might get overwritten by ancient history.*

### ***6\. "Old Man" Silas (The Scrapper)***

* ***The Pitch:** "I ain't got a speech. Get on the boat or don't."*  
* ***The Secret:** Silas has found a "Pre-Stitch" vault in the trash vortex. He needs a crew stupid enough to help him haul it out.*  
* ***The Risk:** Tetanus. Also, the vault might be booby-trapped.*

### ***7\. Captain "Iron-Lung" Jorah (The Submariner)***

* ***The Pitch:** "The surface is a lie. The heat is down deep. Join me in the dark."*  
* ***The Secret:** He is searching for the "Deep-Mother," a mythical creature the Trench-Kin worship.*  
* ***The Risk:** Structural implosion. Total darkness.*

### ***8\. Captain "Iron-Hide" (The Shield)***

* ***The Pitch:** "We don't run. We tank. I bring everyone home, even if I have to carry the ship myself."*  
* ***The Secret:** He isn't looking for a Node; he's field-testing a new "Ablative Hull" made of compressed Ash-blood obsidian.*  
* ***The Loami Hook:** Iron-Hide is a living legend among the Iron-Union. He started as a Copper-rank boiler scrubber (just like Loami). He famously saved the last expedition by manually holding a cracked thermal vent shut with his own geomantic skin for 6 hours. He also wrote "The Percussive Maintenance Guide (Vol. 4)," which is likely the only textbook Loami actually likes.*  
* ***The Risk:** You will take damage. A lot of it. But you won't die.*  
* ***Iggy Hook:** THIS is the boat that first disrupted the tides above the deepkin shelf. It returned regularly and it is how he hitch-hiked to shore (which was massively impressive to do considering the weaponry and defenses)*

## ***üé≤ The 50-Point Boon (Mechanic)***

*When the players choose a Captain, they must "Interview" (Roleplay \+ Roll).*

* ***Great Success (DC 18+):** The Captain grants the **Full Boon (+50 Points)** to the character.*  
  * *Effect: This guarantees Gold Rank for anyone (even Loami).*  
* ***Success (DC 12-17):** The Captain grants a **Partial Boon (+20 Points)**.*  
  * *Effect: Enough to bump a Silver to Gold or a Copper to Silver.*  
* ***Failure (DC \< 12):** The Captain accepts them as "Grunt Labor" (0 Points).*  
  * *Effect: They are just along for the ride. No rank increase.*

***Squad Dilemma:***

*Since **Squad 7-Alpha** must deploy together, they must convince a Captain to take all of them.*

* ***Valentine** won't take Loami (Copper).*  
* ***Ghost** thinks Britt is too loud.*  
* ***Ink** loves Iggy but fears Ignatius will burn his specimens.*  
* ***Silas** will take anyone, but offers no prestige.*

*This forces the players to leverage their connections to get the whole squad hired by a "Good" captain, or settle for the "Bad" one.*
