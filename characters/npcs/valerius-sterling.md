## Overview
Valerius Sterling is the older cousin of [[valentine-sterling|Valentine Sterling]] and a fixture at Vumbua Academy, having been there for several years. He hosts the popular (and possibly unauthorized) radio show that recaps academy events. He is named after his famous uncle, [[valentine-sterling-sr|Valentine Sterling Sr.]].

## Relationships
- **[[valentine-sterling|Valentine Sterling]]**: His younger cousin and the family Scion/Student. Valerius watches over him (and critiques his performance on air).
- **[[valentine-sterling-sr|Valentine Sterling Sr.]]**: His famous uncle and namesake.

## Personality
- **Public Persona:** Arrogant, perfect, and critical of "Variance" (chaos/unpredictability). He often antagonizes the party, calling them "Uncalibrated."
